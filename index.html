Actum Est

Founded in 1996 by Delarvoth, Actum Est is the manifestation of a singular and uncompromising vision, a project forged in solitude and shaped by introspection.

Blending atmospheric and symphonic black metal, Actum Est crafts immersive soundscapes where darkness becomes language. Each composition unfolds like a ritual, where cold melodies and oppressive harmonies converge into a vast, introspective experience.

Every element is conceived and executed as part of a unified expression, raw, deliberate, and deeply personal.

Now unveiled on digital platforms, this release preserves its obscure essence while expanding beyond.

The visual identity of Actum Est was developed with the support of Christophe Szpajdel, reinforcing its connection to the timeless aesthetics of black metal.

Every composition, arrangement, and instrument is crafted solely by the artist, rooted in the raw tradition of the 90s and shaped by dedicated, authentic studio work.

What is done cannot be undone.
